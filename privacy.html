Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2025-06-28

At Nye Data Consulting, your privacy is important to us. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you visit our website or contact us through our online forms.

Information We Collect

  • Information you voluntarily provide via our contact forms, including your name, email address, and message content.
  • Optional file attachments you may choose to send.
  • Technical data such as your IP address and browser type, collected automatically by our hosting platform for security and analytics purposes.

How We Use Your Information

  • To respond to inquiries and provide consulting services.
  • To follow up with you if you express interest in scheduling a call or project work.
  • To improve our website and communication process.

Information Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your information with third parties. We may use trusted service providers (such as Basin for form processing and email providers) who process your data solely to help us operate our business.

Your Consent

By submitting information through our website, you consent to the collection and use of that information in accordance with this policy.

Security

We take appropriate measures to secure the data you submit, including encryption and secure form handling. However, no system is entirely immune from risk. If you have concerns, you may also contact us directly by email at info@nyedata.com.

Your Rights

You may request to access, correct, or delete your personal data at any time by contacting us.

Policy Updates

This policy may be updated periodically. We encourage visitors to review it regularly.
