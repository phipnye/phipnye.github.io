About Nye Data Consulting

Nye Data Consulting provides customized data processing and analysis services, specializing in transforming raw data into actionable insights. I help clients tackle complex data challenges by leveraging advanced tools and techniques such as statistical modeling, machine learning, and data visualization.

With experience across projects involving econometric forecasting, automated data extraction, and interactive visualizations, I focus on delivering efficient solutions that fit your business needs. Whether you need a predictive model, data processing pipeline, or a custom visualization tool, I can help bring clarity to your data.

Nye Data Consulting emphasizes transparent, collaborative partnerships, ensuring that you receive high-quality, tailored solutions from start to finish.

  • Data visualization

  • Custom regression tools and predictive analytics
