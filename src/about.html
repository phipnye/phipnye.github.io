About Nye Data Consulting

Why We Started

Nye Data Consulting was founded to give organizations—especially law firms—a better way to work with data. We saw too many consulting firms prioritize process over value, charging clients for unnecessary meetings and layers of staff. We're here to do things differently: clean, honest work that delivers results without wasting time or money.

What Makes Us Different

At many firms, clients end up paying for junior consultants to learn on the job—often under the guidance of other consultants, all billed at high rates. We don't believe in that. When you hire Nye Data Consulting, you get experienced professionals doing real work from day one. No hidden inefficiencies. No indirect compromise.

We also avoid fluff. No drawn-out meetings, no recycled slide decks, no over-polished reports with little substance. Just clear, direct communication and results you can rely on.

What We Do

We specialize in helping legal teams and other professionals make sense of complicated data. Whether it's analyzing massive claims databases, building litigation-focused statistical models, or automating tedious workflows, we bring technical expertise and strategic thinking to every project.

We've worked with top-tier law firms, litigation experts, and research teams under tight deadlines and high stakes. We're comfortable navigating ambiguity, collaborating with domain specialists, and delivering sharp, defensible insights backed by clear logic.

Our Focus

We don't do management consulting or legal strategy—we do data. And we're really good at it. Our work is grounded in rigorous analysis, reproducible code, and transparent communication. That focus allows us to deliver faster, cleaner, and more effective solutions tailored to your specific needs.

Core Capabilities

  • Data cleaning, integration, and documentation
  • Statistical and predictive modeling (regression, forecasting, simulation)
  • Custom visualizations and interactive tools
  • Scripting, automation, and reproducible workflows in R/Python

Who We Help

While much of our work is in the legal world, we also support researchers, analysts, and small businesses who want to make smarter decisions with their data. If you need a partner who takes your work seriously and treats your time and trust with respect, let's talk.
