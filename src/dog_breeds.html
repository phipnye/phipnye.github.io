This interactive plot showcases a dataset of 160 dog breeds, detailing each breed's friendliness, intelligence, exercise needs, and additional characteristics like size, grooming requirements, and health risks. The plot explores the relationship between friendliness and intelligence ratings, showing variations by breed type, size, and average weight. Each point represents a breed, with color indicating breed type and size denoting average weight. The plot is further divided by size categories to enable comparisons across different breed sizes, offering insights into how breed size may influence temperament and cognitive abilities. Interactive tooltips reveal breed-specific information such as origin, unique traits, and suitability for families, making this visualization a rich resource for exploring breed characteristics. Use the legend to filter by breed type, hover over points to view details, or zoom in to examine specific trends.
