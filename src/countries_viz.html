This interactive globe visualization provides key data on country-specific attributes that reflect economic, social, and environmental aspects across the globe. Users can explore metrics such as population, GDP, surface area, forested area, life expectancy for both males and females, homicide rates, unemployment, and internet usage by hovering over each country. These insights enable a closer look at regional trends and global patterns that impact development and quality of life. The data is sourced from the latest available resources, offering a well-rounded snapshot of global conditions.
